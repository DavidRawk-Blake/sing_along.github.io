Adjusting the lyrics manually

The lyrics can be fine-tuned, and often need to be.

This is because though the AI is really good at finding most words, it can struggle with ends of sentences, and words from specific dialects

For this I open up the lyrics file and listen to the song at the same time...

There's a timestamp in the debug section of the karaoke software that helps find the word errors.
